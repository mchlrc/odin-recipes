Classic Margarita

Description

Margaritas are the perfect drink to go alongside mexican food such as tacos or nachos. They can also be the perfect drink for hanging out by the pool or even kicking back after a long day at the office.

This recipe uses only high quality ingredients that are my favorite. Feel free to substitute your favorite tequila and orange liqueur.

Ingredients

  • 1.5oz Tequila
  • 0.5oz Orange Liqueur
  • 0.5oz Lime Juice
  • 1 Lime Wedge
  • 1 Teaspoon Agave Syrup
  • Kosher Salt
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Rub a lime wedge along the rim of your glass
  2. Dip the rim of the glass on a plate or bowl covered in a thick layer of salt. Move the glass around to get the desired amount of salt to stick to the rim
  3. carefully fill the glass with ice. If you're not careful, salt will be knocked into the glass and onto the cooking surface.
  4. Add ice, tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice, and agave syrup to a cocktail shaker
  5. Shake vigorously
  6. Pour contents of shaker (through the strainer) into the glass
  7. Garnish with a lime wedge or lime wheel
  8. Enjoy
