Taco Meat

Description

Tacos are an easy meal that usually makes everyone happy. Not a lot of people dislike these tasty hand held eats. This taco meat recipe shows just how easy it is to prepare tacos.

This recipe is only focusing on the taco meat aspect of tacos. Toppings and shells/tortillas are up to you! Some suggestions include: Sour Cream , Shredded Cheese, Refried Beans, Hot Sauce, Salsa, Lettuce, and many more!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93% ground beef
  • 1 batch taco seasoning (or packet if using store bought)

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your pan to medium heat
  2. Place your ground beef in the pan and immediately start mashing up the meat with a wooden spoon. Continue mashing and separating until meat is thoroughly broken apart and brown throughout.
  3. pour in taco seasoning and a few ounces of water. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Raise temperature to medium-high heat. Once the mixture comes to a boil reduce heat to low.
  5. Simmer while stirring occasionally until enough water has evaporated to become desired consistency.
